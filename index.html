About The Fest

TechNova 2026 is the premier annual technology festival at East West Institute of Technology, bringing together the brightest minds across the campus. Over two exciting days, immerse yourself in intense coding battles, creative design showcases, technical quizzes, and e-sports tournaments. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, an aspiring innovator, or a fierce competitor, an ultimate challenge awaits you. Join us to learn, innovate, and conquer!

Code Wars

April 8 | 12:00 - 1:00 PM | Room 509, Main Engg Block

Faculty Incharge: Trupthi Ma'am

View Rules
  • 1. Open only to students who have registered in advance
  • 2. Participants can compete individually or in teams of 2
  • 3. The contest will include coding problems of varying difficulty (easy, medium, hard)
  • 4. All problems must be solved within the given time limit
  • 5. Allowed programming languages: C, Java, Python
  • 6. Late submissions after the time ends will not be accepted
  • 7. Use of online resources, AI tools, or any external help is strictly prohibited
  • 8. Participants must use only the assigned systems/platforms
  • 9. In case of a tie, the participant/team with the faster submission time will be ranked higher
  • 10. Decisions made by the judges are final
  • 11. Results will be announced after evaluation
  • 12. Organizers reserve the right to modify the rules if necessary

Quiz

April 8 | 1:00 - 2:30 PM | Room 301, Main Engg Block

Faculty Incharge: Roopa ma'am

View Rules
  • The TECH QUIZ will consist of three rounds:
  • Round 1: Multiple Choice Questions (12 questions, Points gained: 12)
  • Round 2: Unscramble Words (If one team fails, the other can answer. Points: 8)
  • Round 3: Identify the Logo/Image (Time limit is 15 minutes. Press buzzer before answering. Points: 6)
  • General Rules: Buzzer round. Guess within 2 mins. No phones in Round 1.
  • Phones allowed in Round 2 & 3 only for buzzer. Any team member can use it.
  • Points not carried forward. No communication between teams.
  • Be present 10 minutes before start.
