About The Fest

TechNova 2026 is the premier annual technology festival at East West Institute of Technology, bringing together the brightest minds across the campus. Over two exciting days, immerse yourself in intense coding battles, creative design showcases, technical quizzes, and e-sports tournaments. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, an aspiring innovator, or a fierce competitor, an ultimate challenge awaits you. Join us to learn, innovate, and conquer!
